How can I perform an international transfer in Kopra?

assistant:
To perform an **international transfer** in Kopra, follow these steps:
1. **Login** to your Kopra account.
2. Go to the **Payment** menu and select **International Transfer**.
3. Ensure the correct **currency type** and **destination details** are filled in.
4. **Enter the required transaction details**, including the recipient’s account number, amount, and any additional reference information.
5. **Review the transaction** and click **Submit** to initiate the transfer.

For detailed instructions, refer to the **International Transfer section in Buku Panduan Kopra by Mandiri**.

How do I create a Corporate Admin Maker user?

assistant:
To create a **Corporate Admin Maker user** in Kopra, follow these steps:
1. **Login** as SysAdmin Maker to your Kopra account.
2. Navigate to the **Account & User Management** section.
3. Click on **+ADD** to create a new user.
4. **Fill in the mandatory fields**:
   - **User**: Enter the new user's name and user ID.
   - **Gender**: Select the gender of the user.
   - **Email Address**: Enter a valid, active email address for the user.
   - **Mobile Phone**: Enter the user's mobile phone number.
   - **User Group**: Select the user group for the new user.
   - **Role**: Choose **Corporate Admin Maker**.
5. **Configure additional settings**:
   - **View Payroll**: Check if the user should have access to payroll details.
   - **Authentication**: Choose the type of token for authentication (soft or hard token).
6. **Confirm** the details and then **Submit** to complete the user creation process.

For more details, refer to the **Manual Produk K6.MP48 Edisi 1 Revisi 2 Tahun 2024**.

What do I do if I forgot my password and need to reset it?

assistant:
If you have **forgotten your password** and need to reset it, follow these steps:
1. **Login** to your Kopra account as SysAdmin.
2. Navigate to the **Account & User Management** section.
3. Find the **user** whose password needs resetting.
4. Click on **Reset Password** next to their account.
5. **Send a password reset link** to the user's registered **email address**.
6. The user will receive an **email** with instructions to reset their password.
7. Once the password is reset, the user can log in with their new password.

For more details, refer to the **Reset Password section in Buku Panduan Kopra by Mandiri**.

What if I am locked out of my account?

assistant:
If you are **locked out of your account**, follow these steps to regain access:

1. **Check for Error Message**: If your account is locked, you will see a message indicating that it has been temporarily disabled due to multiple failed login attempts.
   
2. **Wait for 90 Days**: Your account will automatically be unlocked after **90 days** if no further login attempts are made. If the issue is urgent:
   
3. **Contact Customer Support**:
   - Call **Bank Mandiri's Call Center** at **14000** for assistance.
   - Alternatively, reach out to your local **BOC Region** or **Branch** to request unlocking your account.
   
4. **Provide Required Information**: Customer support will ask for identification and may require you to complete additional security checks to unlock your account.

5. **Follow Instructions**: Once your account is unlocked, you may need to reset your password or complete security verification.

For further assistance, please contact **Bank Mandiri's support** through the available contact points.

To reset your **locked account**, follow these steps:

5. **Test Login**: Once your account is unlocked, try logging in with your new credentials to ensure everything is functioning correctly.

For additional assistance, refer to the **Locked Account section in Buku Panduan Kopra by Mandiri**.
